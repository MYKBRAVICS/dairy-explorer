🐄 Dairy Explorer — Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. Overview

Dairy Explorer is a static reference guide application for dairy farming professionals and students. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

2. Data We Collect

Dairy Explorer does not collect, store, or share any personal data. The app contains only educational reference content and does not require you to create an account or provide any personal information.

3. Cookies and Tracking

Dairy Explorer does not use cookies or any tracking technologies. No analytics, advertising, or third-party tracking scripts are included in the application.

4. Internet Access

The app uses internet access only to load Google Fonts for display purposes on first launch. After the first visit, the app works fully offline. No personal data is transmitted during this process.

5. Third Party Services

Dairy Explorer does not integrate with any third-party services, social networks, or advertising platforms.

6. Children's Privacy

Dairy Explorer is intended for professional and educational use by adults. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

7. Data Storage

All app data is stored locally on your device only. Nothing is sent to external servers.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
